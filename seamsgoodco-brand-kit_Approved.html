Brand usage rules
The accent rule

Warm Stone (#C8BEA8) only ever appears on two elements: the inner ring and the hairline divider. Never on the main wordmark type. This keeps the mark clean and controlled.

Type hierarchy

SEAMS and GOOD are always the same weight (Raleway 600) and same color (black or white). THE and CO. are always lighter (Raleway 400) and always in the accent color — never black or white.

The double ring

Outer ring is always 2.5px in black or white. Inner ring is always 0.85px in Warm Stone or its tone-matched equivalent. Never reverse the weights or colors.

Natural home

The mark lives most naturally on Linen Cream (#F4F1EC). When in doubt, use cream as your background. White is for digital applications where cream isn't available.

Greek Blue

Reserve for special occasions — premium events, limited merch, holiday campaigns, anniversary materials. Not for everyday use. Keeps it meaningful.

Future brand expansion

As SeamsGoodCo. grows into pool gyms, inflatables, and Porsche sims, the mark stays unchanged. The neutral palette is intentionally category-agnostic — it grows with the brand.
